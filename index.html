Intelligent Integrated Circuits and Systems Lab

We design cutting-edge analog/mixed-signal and RF integrated circuits for biosensing, artificial intelligence, and communications. Our group creates complete microsystems from physical transistor-level concept to silicon, on a single-chip platform. To operate and validate our custom chips, we also develop embedded firmware and software — including MCU-based biosensor readout systems, FPGA-based SNN inference controllers, and on-chip digital control logic.
